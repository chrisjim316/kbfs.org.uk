We help you navigate the world of banking & finance

King’s Banking & Finance Society is the largest finance and banking society at King’s College London. We provide the opportunity for our members to meet top industry professionals, acquire skills through KBFS events and network with like-minded individuals.

Message From The President

We started the year with Australia burning, World War 3 trending and coronavirus as the cherry on our sad 2020 cake. And now before a chance for a breather, the new recruiting season is already upon us. Hence, this year we are working hard to do our part and make the recruitment season as pleasantly easy as possible.

For this year, we are discarding the famous finance-y formalness and keeping things more informal and casual. Over the coming summer months, we will release a series of events and interactions to help you smash that upcoming application season. Do keep an eye out as we have a lot planned for the year. Hosting one of the largest PE megafunds CVC, a three-hour-long bank IB simulation and a masquerade ball in second semester are just some of the events we have for you. Whether you have just ventured into finance, have an interview coming up or want to hear the perspective of a professional, we got you covered! You tell us what you want, and we will do our best to make it happen.

In the meantime, you can join us as a member, follow our social media and let us know if you have any questions. Together let’s make this year bigger and better than ever before!

Our aim is to ‘Help you navigate the world of Banking and Finance’, and we are going to make sure that despite circumstances, you can approach the recruitment season in the most prepared way.

- Harshali Ranjan
   President

Our Mission

Help you navigate the world of banking & finance

King's Banking & Finance Society aims to educate members about the career paths within the banking and finance industry. Our events equip members with essential knowledge to pursue career aspirations in the most competitive graduate job sector.

Our Objectives

We employ a multi-faceted strategy to accomplish our mission:

  • Provide real-world exposure and insight into the banking and finance industry
  • Host networking events to connect students with industry professionals
  • Offer a platform to educate members about the essential skills needed within the industry
