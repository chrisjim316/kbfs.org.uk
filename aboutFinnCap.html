About

  • FinnCap Group provides financial services to growth companies.
  • They are mid-market specialists dedicated to providing the highest quality service to their clients both private and public.
  • They provide quality broking and fundraising capabilities alongside excellence in M&A advisory with a global reach. They have sold over 600 companies to date and are recognised as the largest Adviser on the LSE and No.1 broker on AIM.
  • They always put clients first delivering your business ambition, whether that is to raise growth capital, IPO, refinance, raise debt for your business, execute an acquisition or sell your business.
  • FinnCap's specialist sector knowledge and entrepreneurial approach helps companies to achieve their ambition.
  • FinnCap is listed on AIM and has a team of over 140 people based in London and a strong global reach as a member of Oaklins.
